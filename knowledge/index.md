# knowledge

- [concepts](./concepts/) — Stable ideas: "what is this?".
- [experiments](./experiments/) — Reusable procedures that produce findings when run.
- [findings](./findings/) — Dated, empirical, falsifiable records dumped by agents.
- [guides](./guides/) — Step-by-step playbooks and automation patterns.
- [ideas](./ideas/) — Staging area for draft concepts and experiments.
- [principles](./principles/) — Standards and conventions agreed with humans.
- [reference](./reference/) — Structured reference documentation.
- [structures](./structures/) — How objects are composed and how they work.
